About David McKinney

I build businesses, teams, and strategies—and sometimes even music. My passion for problem-solving started early, shaped by my dad and Jimmy Seals of Seals and Crofts. That creativity now fuels how I help leaders scale, adapt, and thrive.

For over 20 years, I've worked with startups like Prolexic and Zenedge and led at Oracle—helping organizations grow with clear strategy and hands-on execution.

Life doesn't slow down much. With five kids, I've (at least tried to) master the art of balancing priorities. When I do carve out time, you'll find me running, surfing, or on the basketball court.

If you're ready to scale and lead with clarity, let's talk.
